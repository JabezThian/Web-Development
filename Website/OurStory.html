Our Story

At Golden Café, our aim is to match the breath-taking view with quintessential progressive delights, where texture, purity of flavour and quality of ingredients are paramount. Our professional service team and chefs deliver an exceptional dining experience through their knowledge and genuine hospitality that has made Golden Café one of the best fine dining cafés in Singapore.

Golden Café was established in 2002, started by John Smith Williams and James Gorden, both of them are best friends and have two different dreams which could only be made possible if they work together. They both treasured their frienship and worked towards their dream to open a cafe, and that was how The Golden Cafe was established. James Gorden is the MasterChef in 2000 as part of, the café utilises cutting edge nutrition research, ancient wisdom, culinary skills, and international food and nutrition experts to bring you truly healthy and inspiring fare.


Honorary Star Award

The Honorary Star Award looks at the quality of the product; mastery of the cooking technique; balance of flavor; personality of the chef as expressed on the plate; and consistency.

Today, The Golden Cafe have a status of 3 Honorary Star. Back in 2005, The Golden Cafe was the first Cafe in Singapore to earn it's first Honorary Star. Subsequently in 2010, The Golden Cafe earned our second Honorary Star. And just last year (2019) The Golden Cafe managed to earn it's third Honorary Star!


Founder of Golden Cafe

John Smith Williams

I am John Smith Williams from the United States of America. I was born with the ambition to open my very own Cafe. I studied about business in Singapore when I was 17 years old and fell in love with Singapore. I was fortunate to have a best friend (James Gorden) in Singapore who was pursing his dream to be a chef. That was when I decided to open a Cafe in Singapore with him.

Head Chef of Golden Cafe

James Gorden

I am James Gorden from Singapore. When I was as little as 6 years old, I would always help my mother in the kitchen to preapre meals for the family. I've always have the passion and love cooking since then. I grew up having the dream to become the best chef and also being able be a head chef in a kitchen. I was fortunate enough to find John and hear his dream of opening up a cafe in Singapore. That was the moment we both decided to work together to open up this cafe.
